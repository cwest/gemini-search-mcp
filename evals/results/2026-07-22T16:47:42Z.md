## Faithfulness & citations

Faithfulness = supported claims / total (claims checked against fetched source text). Citation precision/recall are ALCE-style. These metrics are only defined on a GROUNDED answer (one that returned sources), so they are conditional metrics: comparing them head-to-head is only valid on cases every model grounded.
